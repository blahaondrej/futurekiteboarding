Non-EU delivery = No VAT

All orders delivered outside the EU will be automatically exempt from VAT deduction (21% of the sales price of the goods). This means that when your goods arrive, you will be obliged to pay only the local charges (import duties, customs fees).

Please note we have no control over custom inspections and the delays caused by them.
