STORY OF
2008s
Once upon a time Tereza met Martin on sunny beaach and tested his boards. She imediately fall in love with the new technology and performance. It didn´t take long and they had founded the company together.
IT ALL STARTED IN
2005
The constuction of the board has been based on the non-standard shape and the two-concaved bottom with sharp edges. The new Futurekiting board showed, since the very first moment of testing, much higher speed than other boards and the speed was kept even during up-winding.
THE FIRST SERIE
PRODUCTION OF
2009
In 2009, after few years spent on testing the kiteboard prototypes, we remained focused on boards for water kiteboarding. We have designed entirely new concept of the 3D bottom shaping – The Fish Belly Technology (FBT) ™. FBT provides absolutely unknown experience of comfort, control, soft landing and a huge pop ability.
WE PRODUCE FUTURE KITE FROM SCRATCH
2013
As we never copy a thing, we have started with intensive development of kite from scratch with world renowned designer Tomas Brauner. Tomas designed unique C- kite which is appreciated for both, stability and performance. “You will probably say” just another kite “Until you try it out,” Mike Blomvall.
