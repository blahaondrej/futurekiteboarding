The story of our brand.

How it all started…

"I think it was 2005, damn what a year that was! Somehow I was not happy with kiteboards on the market. That is where I decided to develop my own!"

MARTIN HAVLÍKFUTURE kiteboarding developer

It all started in…
2005
The construction of the board was based on the non-standard shape and two-concave bottom with sharp edges. Already at the first testing round, the new Future board demonstrated a much higher speed than other boards, and maintained it even during up-winding.
Snowkiting episode
2006
We became more active in the snowkiting arena as well. As always, we decided to go our own way – to design and to build up a snowkite board from scratch and to develop a board reflecting all the aspects a snowkite board requires. Several months in, we could finally come forward with something nobody else had – a special free-style snowkiting board – „RAGE“.
Company being born
2008
Once upon a time, Tereza met Martin on a sunny beach and tested his boards. She immediately fell in love with the new technology and performance. It didn't take long and they founded the company together.
The first production
2009
In 2009, after a few years spent on testing the kiteboard prototypes, we decided to keep focusing on boards for water kiteboarding. We designed an entirely new concept of 3D bottom shaping – The Fish Belly Technology (FBT) ™. FBT provides an absolutely new experience of comfort, control, soft landing and a huge pop ability.
The 2nd generation of our kiteboards
2010
We introduced the F10 kiteboard with a number of new features and improvements. F12 represents the next step of evolution. The boards are becoming widely popular in Europe, Asia and South America.
Introduction of F14 PRO
2013
In 2013, the legendary F14 PRO kiteboard is created, originally designed for the World Cup races. A new concept of kiteboard where high longitudinal stiffness remains while low torsional stiffness is maintained. Positive reactions come from all over the world.
The BOSS is here
2015
After the success of the F14 PRO racing kiteboard, the newly developed BOSS kiteboard is launched. It expands the portfolio with its sports-tuned playful board. This year the decision is made to include a racing line of kites in our portfolio.
Future Wakeboards
2017
After a series of negotiations, cooperation with several other world brands is established and our own OM production of kiteboards is launched.
Kite manufacturing is discontinued. The final portfolio of kiteboards enables us to make a successful entry into the world of wakeboarding.
Partnering up
2019
This year brings a big breakthrough in terms of development. We start establishing a close cooperation with a local SPORTEN factory, now known as KÄESTLE. The result is a significant increase in production capacity and huge technological potential.
The Present day
2021
We have significantly increased production and expanded our portfolio with hydrofoils, mountainboards and skimboards. We are perfectly prepared for future opportunities.
