Czech republic

Marek Zach "Murphy"

Since 2013, we are proud to have a world class kite legend, Marek Murphy Zach in our team. Three times freestyle World Snowkite Champ, Czech Wakeboard Champ. Last 15 years more than 100 standings on the podium. Live like Murphy that's enough.

Germany

Larissa Hofmann

When I started Kitesurfing I didn't think there was a sport that I could ever get that committed to. But after riding my first meters, it was clear I wouldn't ever wanna stop. So I became an Instructor which gave me the chance to travel the world, share my passion for Watersports and work on the nicest spots on earth. From the clear blue seas of Egypt to the Caribbean, to Brazil, to all over Europe, to, of course, my favourite Spot - Lake Garda, and many more.

Netherlands

Daniel Van de Koot

I am based and kiteboarding on the ocean side of Holland, Zandvoort and Ijmuiden. I'm kiting for almost 20 years now and still loving it. Always on the oceanfront when I'm kiting or teaching kite. Why Future boards? I met Future in 2012 in Boracay. Love the innovation of these boards, they're always helpful to bring me up to the next level. I like big air, oldschool and big kiteloops.. no matter how fast I come down, the board catches me always how fast I go.
